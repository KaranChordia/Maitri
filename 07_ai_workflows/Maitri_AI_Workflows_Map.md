# Maitri AI Workflows Map

This document maps practical AI workflows for Maitri across internal operations and client-facing/community experiences.

## Operating Principle

AI should help Maitri move faster while preserving four non-negotiables:

- Historical accuracy.
- Child-safe language and age appropriateness.
- Brand warmth and cultural sensitivity.
- Human approval before publication.

## Workflow 1 - Character Universe Builder

### Purpose

Turn approved research into a consistent character dossier for every Maitri heroine.

### Inputs

- Historical research notes.
- Approved sources.
- Target age range.
- Region, era, clothing, language, and value themes.

### AI Output

- Character card.
- Personality profile.
- Story conflict ideas.
- Visual palette suggestions.
- Parent discussion prompts.
- Child activity ideas.

### Human Review

- Historical reviewer.
- Founder/brand reviewer.
- Child language reviewer.

## Workflow 2 - Story Development Assistant

### Purpose

Create first drafts and structured story arcs while keeping the final voice human-edited.

### Inputs

- Character dossier.
- Chapter structure.
- Core lesson.
- Age band.

### AI Output

- Chapter outline.
- Scene beats.
- Dialogue options.
- Illustration prompts.
- End-of-book activities.

### Guardrails

- Do not invent historical facts without flagging them.
- Separate fiction from documented history.
- Avoid glorifying violence.
- Keep emotional complexity but use child-safe framing.

## Workflow 3 - Historical Claim Tracker

### Purpose

Prevent factual drift as stories, captions, workshops, and newsletters are created.

### Inputs

- Draft content.
- Approved source list.

### AI Output

- Claims that need citation.
- Possible inaccuracies.
- Fictionalized moments to label clearly.
- Suggested reviewer questions.

### Human Review

A qualified historian or subject-matter reviewer must approve final historical claims.

## Workflow 4 - Social Content Engine

### Purpose

Generate repeatable content from each character without losing brand consistency.

### Inputs

- Character dossier.
- Monthly theme.
- Platform: Instagram, YouTube Shorts, newsletter, parent community.

### AI Output

- Reel scripts.
- Carousel copy.
- Caption variants.
- Hooks.
- Parent prompts.
- Story snippets.
- Hashtag sets.

### Suggested Content Families

- What would Manu do?
- Before she was famous.
- Courage in small moments.
- One Indian word.
- Parent-child question of the week.
- Behind the doll.
- Guess the heroine.

## Workflow 5 - Newsletter Personalization

### Purpose

Make each waitlist subscriber feel like they are entering the Maitri world.

### Segments

- Parent of age 3-5 child.
- Parent of age 6-8 child.
- Parent of age 9-11 child.
- NRI family.
- Educator.
- Gift buyer.

### AI Output

- Welcome sequence.
- Character letters.
- Activity prompts by age.
- Beta-reader invitations.
- Preorder readiness messages.

## Workflow 6 - Beta Reader Feedback Analyzer

### Purpose

Convert parent and child feedback into product and story decisions.

### Inputs

- Survey responses.
- Read-aloud notes.
- Parent interviews.
- Child quotes.

### AI Output

- Common likes.
- Confusing parts.
- Emotional moments that worked.
- Age-fit issues.
- Parent objections.
- Suggested story edits.
- Demand signal summary.

## Workflow 7 - School Workshop Kit Generator

### Purpose

Turn Maitri characters into school-ready sessions.

### Inputs

- Character story.
- Grade level.
- Session duration.
- Learning objective.

### AI Output

- Workshop plan.
- Teacher script.
- Student worksheet.
- Group activity.
- Take-home parent prompt.
- Follow-up email.

### Example Workshop

Title: What Does Courage Look Like?

Character: Manu

Age: 6-9

Outcome: Children identify one brave action they can take in daily life.

## Workflow 8 - Waitlist And Preorder Intelligence

### Purpose

Validate demand before inventory investment.

### Inputs

- Waitlist form answers.
- Email behavior.
- Survey data.
- Preorder interest.
- Price feedback.

### AI Output

- Segment profiles.
- Top objections.
- Character demand ranking.
- Price comfort bands.
- Recommended launch sequence.
- Campaign performance summary.

## Workflow 9 - Compliance And Production Knowledge Base

### Purpose

Keep regulatory, supplier, certification, and material decisions organized.

### Inputs

- BIS requirements.
- Supplier updates.
- Material specifications.
- Test reports.
- Production timelines.

### AI Output

- Compliance checklist.
- Missing document alerts.
- Parent-friendly safety explanations.
- Production FAQ drafts.
- Risk log.

### Important Guardrail

AI can track and summarize compliance information, but it cannot replace legal, regulatory, or testing certification advice.

## Workflow 10 - Client-Facing Character Quiz

### Purpose

Help families discover which Maitri character their child may connect with first.

### Inputs

- Child interests.
- Preferred activities.
- Current challenge.
- Parent-selected value focus.

### AI Output

- Character match.
- Short reason.
- Story chapter recommendation.
- Parent-child activity.
- Waitlist CTA.

### Example Result

Your child may love Manu because she asks big questions, tries hard things, and learns that courage can be both bold and kind.

## Recommended First Build

Start with four lightweight workflows before building anything complex:

1. Character Universe Builder.
2. Social Content Engine.
3. Beta Reader Feedback Analyzer.
4. Waitlist And Preorder Intelligence.

These directly support the current business need: create buzz, build community, and validate demand before inventory.
