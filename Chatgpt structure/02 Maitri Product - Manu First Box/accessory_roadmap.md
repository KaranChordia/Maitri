# Maitri Add-On Revenue Roadmap

## Principle

The story should create the demand for accessories.

Accessories should feel like natural ways to continue the child's relationship with Manu, not random merchandise attached to a doll.

## Launch Sequence

### First Box

The first box proves the core experience:

- Doll.
- Storybook.
- Letter.
- Activities.
- Stickers.

Only after this is validated should Maitri expand into add-ons.

## Tier 1 - Small Add-Ons

Indicative price band: Rs. 299-799

Purpose:

Low-friction parent purchases and gift add-ons.

Ideas:

- Sticker packs.
- Flash cards.
- Additional activity books.
- Character journals.
- Dress sets.
- Hair accessories.
- Mini pets.

Best early candidates:

1. Manu sticker pack.
2. Courage journal.
3. What Would Manu Do? cards.
4. Simple outfit or hair accessory pack.

Why:

These extend the story without needing heavy manufacturing complexity.

## Tier 2 - Play Sets

Indicative price band: Rs. 999-2,499

Purpose:

Increase imaginative play and margin once the character is loved.

Ideas:

### Manu's Warrior Kit

- Sword.
- Shield.
- Cape.

Guardrail:

Keep the emotional message on courage, responsibility, and protection. Avoid making aggression the play pattern.

### Manu's Horse

- Badal figure.
- Saddle.
- Grooming brush.

Why this may be stronger than the warrior kit:

Book 1 centers the horse relationship, so a Badal add-on is directly story-led.

## Tier 3 - Premium Sets

Indicative price band: Rs. 2,999-5,999

Purpose:

Higher-value gifting after the brand proves trust.

Ideas:

### Jhansi Fort Playset

Possible components:

- Fort walls.
- Gates.
- Throne room.
- Secret passages.

Manufacturing note:

Start with foldable cardboard or lightweight construction before investing in a complex molded playset.

## Educational Add-On

### Build A Kingdom Map Game

Potential learning themes:

- Leadership.
- Resource allocation.
- Geography.
- Problem solving.
- Cooperation.

Why it is distinctive:

It gives Maitri a learning product beyond doll play and ties well to Manu's later story ladder.

## Validation Before Production

Before producing each add-on, test:

- Does the child ask for this after the story?
- Does the parent understand the educational or play value?
- Can it be made safely and durably?
- Does it support the character arc?
- Does the price band feel reasonable?

## Recommended Add-On Order

1. Sticker pack.
2. Courage journal.
3. What Would Manu Do? cards.
4. Badal horse figure.
5. Manu outfit or hair accessory pack.
6. Build A Kingdom Map Game prototype.
7. Jhansi Fort Playset concept.

## Items To Delay

Delay these until demand is clearer:

- Large fort playset.
- Multiple outfits at once.
- Multi-character accessory bundles.
- App-connected products.
- Large school kits.
- Premium collector editions.
