# Maitri Foundation Map

Last updated: 2026-06-15

## Current Foundation Principle

Maitri should stay narrow before it scales.

The immediate foundation is:

1. Build emotional attachment to Manu.
2. Prove parents see educational and cultural value.
3. Validate demand before heavier inventory, school, preorder, or ecosystem expansion.
4. Keep future revenue streams organized as planned add-ons, not first-launch dependencies.

## Workspace Map

### 00_project_admin

Use for operating maps, status, meeting notes, and current working priorities.

Key files:

- `project_status.md`
- `monthly_retainer_foundation_plan.md`
- `maitri_foundation_map.md`

### 01_strategy

Use for brand strategy, execution plans, launch decisions, and client-facing strategic rationale.

Recommended subfolders:

- `brand_positioning/`
- `execution_plans/`
- `launch_decisions/`
- `client_proposals/`

### 02_character_universe

Use for character bibles, source stories, story arcs, historical notes, and repeatable character templates.

Current priority:

- Manu remains the first universe anchor.
- New characters should not be developed deeply until Manu's first box, storybook, and validation path are clear.

### 03_content

Use for audience-facing content that builds the world before product launch.

Content should be derived from approved character and product strategy, not invented separately.

Current content lanes:

- Social media.
- Story newsletters.
- Historical storytelling.

### 04_community

Use for beta readers, parent community, and school-workshop planning.

This should activate after the first story and box direction are strong enough to test with families.

### 05_waitlist_preorder

Use for interest capture, waitlist flows, founder-circle planning, and preorder readiness.

Rule:

Capture interest now. Do not request payment or promise timelines until product readiness is reviewed.

### 06_demand_validation

Use for signal reports, survey interpretation, family testing, pricing questions, and launch-readiness evidence.

This folder should answer:

- Are parents interested?
- Are children attached to Manu?
- What objections repeat?
- What must be fixed before paid preorder?

### 07_ai_workflows

Use for internal workflow maps, prompts, and assisted review systems.

AI should support speed and structure, but human review is required for history, child language, product claims, and founder approval.

### 08_assets

Use for source visuals, doll samples, brand references, and approved image assets.

Keep source/reference material visible here. Runtime website copies may live separately inside demo or app folders.

### 09_demos

Use for website prototypes and deployable demos.

Keep demos clearly separated from strategy and product source files.

### 10_source_context

Use for original founder notes, emails, reference scans, and raw source context.

Founder notes should be preserved here before being translated into strategy or product documents.

### 11_product_development

Use for the physical product, box experience, storybook packaging, accessories, manufacturing assumptions, compliance notes, and pricing/cost thinking.

This is the new home for Shwetika's first-box approach.

Recommended subfolders:

- `first_doll_box/`
- `add_on_revenue/`
- `manufacturing_readiness/`
- `product_testing/`

## Decision Flow

Use this order when adding new work:

1. Source context goes into `10_source_context/`.
2. Strategic interpretation goes into `01_strategy/`.
3. Character canon goes into `02_character_universe/`.
4. Product packaging and accessories go into `11_product_development/`.
5. Public-facing content goes into `03_content/`.
6. Community tests go into `04_community/`.
7. Survey and signal analysis goes into `06_demand_validation/`.
8. Website execution goes into `09_demos/`.

## Near-Term Focus

For the next phase, Maitri should not add more surface area. The work should converge around four documents:

1. Manu character bible.
2. Manu 32-page storybook blueprint.
3. First doll box blueprint.
4. Monthly signal report.

If those four become strong, the rest of the ecosystem can scale with less confusion.
