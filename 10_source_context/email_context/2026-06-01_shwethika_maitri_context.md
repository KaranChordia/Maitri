# Shwethika Maitri Context

## Summary

Shwethika wants help building Maitri Dolls as a story-led brand and community before the dolls are available for sale. The project is currently stalled by import restrictions on parts from China and compliance certifications, but she expects progress to improve from June.

## Core Direction

Maitri Dolls is a collection of 18-inch companion dolls inspired by remarkable women from India's history and present day. Each doll represents courage, curiosity, compassion, resilience, leadership, and the many ways women have shaped India.

The goal is to build emotional connection with the characters before people see or buy the dolls.

## Presence To Build

- Instagram
- YouTube Shorts
- Story newsletters
- Parent community
- Beta readers for books
- School workshops
- Character reveals
- Historical storytelling content

## Requested Strategic Assets

- Complete brand positioning strategy
- Character universe bible
- Pre-launch community-building roadmap
- Social media content strategy for the next 6 months
- Waitlist and preorder strategy
- Roadmap to validate demand before investing heavily in inventory

## Product Notes

Each doll is expected to feature:

- Non-toxic, BPA-free silicone limbs for a soft, skin-like touch
- Plush torso made of cotton and fabric for comfort and safety
- Long, combable hair suitable for styling
- Varied features, skin tones, and hair types representing Indian regional diversity
- A storybook explaining the life of the represented character
- Worksheets or activities
